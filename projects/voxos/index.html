The Inspiration

Jillian Kowalchuk, CEO and Founder
Safe and The City

The idea for the app came after CEO and Founder, Jillian Kowalchuk, experienced sexual harassement herself: "I put a lot of trust into navigation apps to get me around London, but found navigating only the fastest route wasn't necessarily the safest. One evening I was guided down a dark, narrow, empty alley, where two male kitchen staff on break said vulgar acts about what they wanted to do to my body. I ignored them, put on a brave face, but felt scared knowing I was walking where I couldn't easily escape or seek help. Their threats turned out to be empty, but I started thinking about younger or more vulnerable persons walking there where the situation could have escalated. With Safe and The City I want to empower others to have all the information to help them feel and actually be safer".

Maja Koeberl, Co-Founder
Voxos Smartglasses

Co-Founder Maja witnessed a woman next to her on a station platform getting hit by a train and flying across the platform. The woman was disconnected from her surroundings and could not hear the approaching train, she ended up with serious head injuries. This is what drove Maja to research and develop a solution to ensure this does not happen again to others and how she discovered bone conduction technology. She never liked the look and feel of bone conduction headphones, especially because they can't be used together with glasses, so she worked with Sanjay and her team to creat Voxos, to provide everyone with an alternative to headphones that is stylish, functional and safe.

Voxos is a pair of glasses that uses bone conduction technology to transfer sound from a mobile device directly to the inner ear without blocking the natural hearing sense.

Bone conduction is the process by which sound waves are transmitted to the inner ear by the cranial bones without traveling through the air in the ear canal.

Vibrations released from the glasses send sound waves through the skull directly to the inner ear.

Urban areas require us to be aware of our surroundings to avoid dangerous situations. Navigating to a specific destination at night brings a certain risk, especially for women.

Voxos enables you to receive the navigations for the safest route from the Safe and The City app without blocking your hearing sense. This enables you to travel home safely.
